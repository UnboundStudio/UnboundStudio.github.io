Process

Our workflow to make the work flow.

Step 1

Project Request

At the beginning of our collaboration is the project request. Your information helps us to understand if we am the right people for your project. If yes, we will arrange a meeting so we can learn more.

Step 2

First Meeting

Trust is extremely important to us when working together. In our initial meeting, we get to know each other and see if we are a good fit. We also talk about the exact goals & requirements, clarify questions and define the next steps.

Step 3

Proposal

After we have learned as much as possible about your project, We create an individual proposal. We don't charge for our work by the hour, but on a per project basis. This way you have a full overview of the costs.

Step 4

Implementation

As soon as we have the required materials, we start with implementation. We place particular emphasis on transparency, convenient communication channels (like Slack) and regular updates.

Step 5

Launch / Handover

Before the launch, the finished website goes through an internal quality assurance in which it is put through its paces. Depending on the project, the website will be hosted via our ultra-fast hosting or integrated into another environment via export.

Frequently asked questions

What services do you offer exactly?

Our focus is on web development and designing based on your niche. Additionally, we offer consulting services during your design phase with our expertise in Branding.

How much does a project cost?

Each project has different needs and requirements. We help you determine the cost of your project and also help you cut down on unnecessary charges.

Which companies do you work with?

We work with corporations or individuals who are looking to take their initiative to the next level.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. We primarily based in India & New York and have clients in the all parts of the globe.

What are the payment terms for a project?

50% upfront and 50% after launch. However, this payment schedule can be adjusted. For international transfers, most of our clients use Wise.

How long does a project take?

Most of our projects can take anywhere between 2 weeks and 2 months. We make sure to keep in touch with you throughout the project, provide you with updates and deliver in time.
